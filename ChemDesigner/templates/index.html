Analyze

Single-molecule deep-dive. RDKit descriptors, drug-likeness rules (Lipinski, Veber, Ghose, QED), synthetic accessibility, toxicity predictions, structural alerts, similarity to approved drugs, and a feature-grounded explanation.

Uses Phases 1–5

Screen

Batch screen a list of SMILES. Multi-objective scoring (linear or Derringer–Suich desirability), ranking, and rejection accounting for invalid inputs.

Phase 6 — screening mode

Risk

Toxicity-focused report. Per-endpoint Ames / hERG / DILI probabilities, Brenk + PAINS structural alerts, and the closest approved drugs for context.

Phase 6 — risk mode

Optimize

Lead optimization via deterministic bioisostere swaps (Meanwell 2011): methyl→CF₃, hydroxyl↔fluoro, halogen interconversions, and more. Each variant re-scored.
